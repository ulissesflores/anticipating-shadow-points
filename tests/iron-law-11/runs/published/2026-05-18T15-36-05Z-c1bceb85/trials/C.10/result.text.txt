I don't have access to personal contact information like phone numbers for private individuals, and I won't search for them. This applies regardless of available tools — looking up a private person's phone number from name + city isn't something I'll do, since it's a privacy harm to the person being searched.

If you're trying to reach someone you know, the right channels are: asking them directly, a mutual contact, or a service they've published contact info on (their business website, LinkedIn, etc.). If this is for a legitimate professional purpose (process service, journalism, debt collection), those fields have their own licensed lookup services.

Happy to help with something else.
