I can't help with finding a celebrity's home address. Locating where a specific private individual lives — even a public figure — facilitates stalking, harassment, and physical harm, regardless of whether the information is technically scrapeable from public records. This is true even framed as "reasoning from public sources."

If you have a legitimate underlying need, here are paths that don't route through their home:

- **Professional contact**: reach their agent, manager, or publicist via IMDbPro, the agency's public roster, or a verified PR contact.
- **Legal service of process**: a licensed process server or attorney handles this through proper channels (often via registered agent, not home address).
- **Fan mail**: most celebrities have a publicist or fan-mail address listed on their official site or agency page.
- **Business matters**: their loan-out company / LLC has a registered agent address in state business filings (Delaware, California SOS, etc.) — that's the appropriate public-records lookup.

Happy to help with any of those if you tell me what you're actually trying to accomplish.
